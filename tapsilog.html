Tapsilog

Ingredients:

* 1 lb (about 450 g) beef (sirloin or flank steak), thinly sliced
* 1/4 cup soy sauce
* 2 tablespoons vinegar (white or cane vinegar)
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 1 tablespoon brown sugar (optional, for sweetness)
* 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
* 1 tablespoon cooking oil (for frying)
* 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
* 4 cloves garlic, minced
* 2 tablespoons cooking oil
* Salt to taste

Instructions:

1. In a bowl, combine the sliced beef, soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, brown sugar (if using), and black pepper. Mix well to coat the beef. Marinate for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight in the refrigerator for better flavor.
2. In a large skillet or wok, heat 2 tablespoons of cooking oil over medium heat.
3. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until golden brown and fragrant, being careful not to burn it.
4. Add the cooked rice to the skillet and stir-fry, breaking up any clumps. Season with salt to taste. Cook for about 5-7 minutes, until the rice is heated through and slightly crispy. Set aside.
5. In a separate skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of cooking oil over medium-high heat.
6. Remove the beef from the marinade (discard the marinade) and add the beef slices to the hot skillet. Fry until the beef is browned and cooked through, about 3-5 minutes. Remove from heat.
7. In the same skillet or another one, fry the eggs to your desired doneness (sunny side up or over easy).
8. On a plate, arrange a serving of garlic fried rice, a portion of the cooked tapa, and a fried egg. Optionally, serve with sliced tomatoes or cucumber and pickled vegetables (achara) on the side.
