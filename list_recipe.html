Adobo

A classic Filipino dish, adobo features marinated meat—commonly chicken or pork—slow-cooked in a savory blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and black pepper. The result is tender, flavorful meat in a rich, dark sauce, often served with steamed rice.

Tapsilog

A popular Filipino breakfast dish, tapsilog consists of three main components: tapa (marinated and pan-fried beef), sinangag (garlic fried rice), and a fried egg. This hearty meal is often accompanied by pickled vegetables or a side of vinegar for dipping, making it a satisfying way to start the day.

Tinola

Tinola is a comforting Filipino chicken soup made with tender pieces of chicken simmered in a flavorful broth with ginger, garlic, and onions. It typically includes vegetables like green papaya or chayote and fresh spinach or chili leaves, creating a light yet nourishing dish that is perfect for any occasion.
